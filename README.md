The overall objective could be a CTA Station explorer. The purpose of this would be for the user to overlook the L stations, and be given a multitude of data that could possibly represent ridership numbers, which ‘L’ lines, station popularity, station backups/delays, maybe accessibility as well. This is to be narrowed down and added a focus and purpose for each data instead of these vague ideas.


Data:

Stations - The chosen area in the whole track to observe the ridership and frequency of use
Ridership - The overall extent of how much a station and specific ‘L’ line is used at this current instance of observation, providing an overall value.
Lines - The ‘L’ line, will give information on where the train is coming from, but also which area of the city and what neighborhoods and demographics will frequently use the train.
Busses - Could provide an overview of the importance of a line, by showcasing the frequency it is offloading bus passengers, and use to gauge the “importance” of a station.
Miscellaneous Data - Data/Metrics not immediately available or recorded such as average transit time between stations, number of “ghost” or delayed trains
